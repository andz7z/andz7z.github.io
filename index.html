My Journey

2023

University Beginnings

I started university without knowing exactly what to expect, but one thing was certain - I wanted to succeed! It was tough at first, especially since I came from a natural sciences high school, not a computer science one. But here I am now.

2024

The Spark of Creation

I started with small applications in a language called LUA. I was playing GTA: San Andreas and couldn’t stop creating little mods that enhanced the user experience - from simple skin changers to a full Season Changer mod that completely transformed the map, UI, and overall feel of the game.

I began to develop a clear vision of what I wanted to do. I found myself drawn to languages like Python, HTML/CSS, and JavaScript. However, I didn’t know where to start — there was so much to learn. So, I began with Python, creating small projects such as a store inventory application.
